Elite Rentals Service Agreement

1. Service Agreement

Welcome to Elite Rentals. By using our luxury car rental services, you agree to these terms and conditions. Our service provides premium vehicle rentals with exceptional customer experience.

2. Rental Requirements

  • Must be 21+ years old with valid driver's license
  • Valid credit card for security deposit
  • Clean driving record (no major violations in past 3 years)
  • Proof of insurance or purchase our insurance package

3. Vehicle Usage

  • Vehicles must be returned in the same condition as received
  • No smoking, pets, or illegal activities in vehicles
  • Mileage restrictions may apply to certain premium vehicles
  • Fuel must be returned at the same level as pickup

4. Pricing & Payment

  • Daily rates as displayed on booking confirmation
  • Security deposit: $500-$2000 depending on vehicle
  • Late return fees: $50/hour after grace period
  • Damage assessment based on independent evaluation

5. Cancellation Policy

  • Free cancellation up to 24 hours before pickup
  • 50% refund for cancellation within 24 hours
  • No refund for no-shows or same-day cancellations
  • Weather-related cancellations handled case-by-case

6. Insurance & Liability

Renters are responsible for all damage, theft, or loss during rental period. Our comprehensive insurance packages are available at competitive rates.

7. Contact Information
